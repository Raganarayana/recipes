About Maggie:

    Ingrediants
  • Maggie 2min Noodles
  • Tomato Kecthup
  • oil
  • cumin seeds
  • Tomato (Finely chopped)
  • Onion (Finely chopped)
  • peas
  • Salt

    • Procedure

    1. Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list
    2. Heat the oil and then rost the cumin seeds in it
    3. Then,toss in the tomatoes,the onions and cook them well
    4. Add the peas and the Maggie Tomato Kecthup,give it a stir, and let them cook for a while!
    5. Cook one pack of MAGGI masala noodles and transfer it onto a dish
    6. Put the cumin-tomato-onion mix right on top of the MAGGI noodles and you are ready to serve

    About Upma:

      Ingrediants
  • Rava
  • Aromatics
  • Lentils
  • Nuts
  • Spices
  • Herbs
  • Onions(finely chopped)
  • Green Chillies(chopped)
  • Ginger(finely chopped)
  • Coriander leaves
  • Curry leaves
  • Cashews
  • Chana dal
  • Urad dal

    • Procedure

    1. heat a pan or kadai first
    2. add 1 cup rava or cream of wheat
    3. begin to roast the rava
    4. stir often while roasting the rava
    5. rava should become fragrant and start to look dry,separate and crisp
    6. don't brown the rava
    7. once the rava becomes fragrant and starts to look dry and crisp,switch off the flame
    8. put the roasted rava on a plate and set aside
    9. in a pan,heat 2 tablespoons ghee
    10. lower the heat
    11. add 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
    12. when you hear the crackling sound of mustard seeds,it means getting fried
    13. now add 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    14. 1 teaspoon chana dal and urad dal
    15. stirring often fry until the chana dal and urad dal begin to brown a bit
    16. immediately add 10 to 12 cashews and begin to fry on a low to medium low heat
    17. by the time the cashews get golden the dals should also be golden
    18. now add the 1/3 cup of finely chopped onions
    19. saute the onions until they soften and become translucent on a low to medium-low heat
    20. then add 1 teaspoon chopped green chillies,1 teaspoon chopped gingerband 8 to 10 curry leaves
    21. mix well and saute for a few seconds
    22. then add 2.5 cups water to this mixture
    23. add salt as required and mix well
    24. stir again on a medium to high flame ,heat the water and let it come to a rolling boling
    25. when water comes to a rooling boil,lower the flame to its lowest
    26. then add the rava in 4 to 5 batches with a spoon
    27. once you add the first batch of rava, stir and mix immediately
    28. continue to add and stir the rava up to last batch
    29. cover and allow the rava upma to steam for 2 to 3 minutes on a low heat
    30. trun of heat and ravais cooked well
    31. upma is ready

    About Dosa

      ingredients
  • Grain rice
  • Urad dal
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Onion(chopped)
  • Green chillies(chopped)
  • Ghee
  • Black mustard seeds
  • Chana dal
  • Sprig curry leaves
  • Tumeric powder
  • Potatotes
  • cocount,cilantro and tomato chutney

    • procedure

    1. soak rice and urad dal in separate bowls for 4-6 hours
    2. process rice and urad dal mixture in commerical grinder
    3. while grinding add water a cup at a time until it reaches the consistency of pancake batter
    4. salt to taste
    5. cover mixture and let sit over night
    6. do not rergigertate
    7. add oil to a skillet
    8. shake mixture over the flame until golden brown
    9. add curry leaves,green leaves and onions
    10. sprinkle turmeric powder and salt ,stir
    11. break up boiled potato into small bowl with a flat bottom
    12. pour refrigerated dosa mixture into a small bowl with flat bottom
    13. pour batter into a greased skillet
    14. immediately,starting from the center,begin forming a circular shape
    15. brush on ghee to frying dosa
    16. place filling near the center of dosa
    17. lightly lift the edges of the dosa
    18. begin rolling from the edge of dosa as wrap
    19. remove dosa from hot top
